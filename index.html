Meet the Mind Behind Sprungwest Tutoring

I’m Manish—a seasoned project and product management professional by trade and an educator at heart. My journey into tutoring began in the most personal way: helping my own child navigate struggles in Math and Physics. Watching them gain confidence and excel ignited a spark in me. Soon, friends and neighbors began noticing the results and asked me to teach their children too. That’s when I realized my true calling—and Sprungwest Tutoring Services was born.

At Sprungwest, my philosophy is simple:

  • No Question is Dumb: Every question matters and curiosity drives success.
  • Beyond the Textbook: I teach students to see Math and Physics as tools to understand the real world—not just numbers and formulas.
  • Fun Meets Fundamentals: Learning should be engaging, not intimidating.

    • What truly sets me apart is my focus on building deep conceptual understanding, strong problem-solving skills, and critical thinking. I believe grades are merely the byproduct of effort and comprehension—when you focus on truly understanding the subject, the marks will take care of themselves.

    If you’re ready to see Math and Physics in a whole new light, with a tutor who’s as invested in your success as you are—welcome to Sprungwest Tutoring!
